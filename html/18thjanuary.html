Siamese Cat:
  1. Origin:Thailand (formerly Siam)
  2. Habits: Siamese cats are known for their vocal nature and deep blue almond-shaped eyes. They are highly social and thrive on human interaction.
  3. Habitat: Adaptable to various environments, Siamese cats enjoy cozy spots with a view, as they love to observe their surroundings.

Bengal Cat:
  1. Origin: United States (with Asian leopard cat ancestry)
  2. Habits: Bengals are known for their distinctive spotted or marbled coat and active, playful nature. They love climbing and exploring.
  3. Habitat: Bengals enjoy vertical spaces and need mental stimulation. Cat trees and interactive toys are ideal for their energetic personalities.
  4. Favorite Foods: A high-protein diet, resembling their ancestral carnivorous diet, suits Bengals best.
